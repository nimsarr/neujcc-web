What We Do!

 We are the Japanese Culture Club at Northeastern University. Our aim is to help everyone experience numerous aspects of Japanese culture, while having fun and making new friends on the way!
 We have weekly meetings every Tuesday and Friday at 6:00pm in Ell Hall Room 408. We celebrate various Japanese holidays, host several cooking sessions (such as sushi, onigiri, takoyaki, soba, and so on), watch a variety of Japanese movies, play traditional board games and video games, as well as 練ぅ日本語 (practice Japanese).
